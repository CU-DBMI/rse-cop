A curated set of resources for Research Software Engineers at CU Anschutz and beyond.

## Community and events

- [Conferences](/conferences/) — a curated list of RSE and domain-specific conferences, workshops, and community events worth attending or submitting to.
- [How to Run an RSE-CoP Meeting](/run-rse-cop-meeting/) — checklist for scheduling, room booking, invitations, slides, agenda issues, and lightning talks.

## External references

- [US-RSE](https://us-rse.org/) — the US Research Software Engineer Association; working groups, job board, mentoring, and an annual conference.
- [The Turing Way](https://the-turing-way.netlify.app/) — community handbook for reproducible, ethical, and collaborative research computing.
- [Software Carpentry](https://software-carpentry.org/lessons/) / [Data Carpentry](https://datacarpentry.org/lessons/) — peer-reviewed, community-developed lessons for foundational research computing skills.
- [CaRCC RCD Professionalization](https://carcc.org/rcd-professionalization/facings/) — frameworks for Research Computing and Data roles and career paths.
- [ACCESS](https://access-ci.org/) — training materials and compute allocations for US research computing.
